Billing

Manage or cancel your subscription

Best Friend Premium is billed securely through your Apple account. You're always in control.

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions.
  3. Select Best Friend from your list of subscriptions.
  4. Choose a different plan, or tap Cancel Subscription.

Cancelling stops future renewals — you'll keep Premium until the end of your current billing period. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, open the app and tap Restore Purchases on the Premium screen.

Quick answers

Download Best Friend from the App Store, open the Premium screen and tap to start your 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before then.
