Billing

Manage or cancel your subscription

Best Friend Premium is billed securely through your Google Play account. You're always in control.

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  2. Tap your profile icon, then tap Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Select Best Friend from your list of subscriptions.
  4. Choose a different plan, or tap Cancel subscription.

Cancelling stops future renewals — you'll keep Premium until the end of your current billing period. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, sign in with the same Google account and your subscription restores automatically.
